About ralph

The story revolves around Ralph, the villain of an arcade game called "Fix-It Felix Jr." Tired of being the bad guy and not receiving any recognition, Ralph sets out on a journey to prove he can be a hero. He ventures through various arcade games, meeting different characters, and ultimately trying to earn a hero's medal. Along the way, he befriends Vanellope von Schweetz, a glitchy character from the candy-themed racing game "Sugar Rush," and together they face numerous challenges.
